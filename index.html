🎮 HexBlast – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2026  |  Last Updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how HexBlast ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile puzzle game HexBlast (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

HexBlast is designed to work without requiring you to create an account or provide personal information. The following data may be collected:

  • Game Progress Data: Your level completion status, coins, powerups, lives, and daily reward streak are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.
  • Optional Ad Data: If you choose to watch optional ads (to refill lives or earn coins), our ad provider may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. Ads are never shown mid-gameplay without your consent.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected automatically by the Google Play platform for crash reporting and performance monitoring.

2. How We Use Information

  • Save your game progress locally on your device
  • Provide optional ad-based rewards when you choose to watch an ad
  • Monitor app stability and fix crashes via Google Play's built-in reporting

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and optional crash reporting. Google's Privacy Policy
  • AdMob (Google): Used to serve optional video ads when you choose to watch them. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers. You can opt out in your device settings under Google → Ads. AdMob Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

All game data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not operate any servers that store your personal data.

  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete your local game data
  • We cannot recover your game progress if your device is lost or reset
  • No account login is required or available

5. Children's Privacy

HexBlast is suitable for players of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require registration, account creation, or submission of any personal data to play.

Optional ads are configured to comply with COPPA and Google Play's Families Policy.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Reset your game progress at any time by uninstalling and reinstalling the App
  • Opt out of personalized ads via your device's Google settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization)

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
